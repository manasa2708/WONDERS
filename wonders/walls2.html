Christ The Redeemer :

Christ the Redeemer : Christ the Redeemer (Portuguese: Cristo Redentor, standard Brazilian Portuguese: ['k istu ed'to ], loca l pronunciation: ['k i tu xe to]) is an Art Deco statue of Jesus Christ in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, created b y French sculptor Paul Landowski and built by Brazilian engineer Heitor da Silva Costa, in collaboration with Fre nch engineer Albert Caquot. Romanian sculptor Gheorghe Leonida fashioned the face. Constructed between 1922 and 1931, the statue is 30 metres (98 ft) high, excluding its 8-metre (26 ft) pedestal. The arms stretch 28 metres (92 ft) wide.
